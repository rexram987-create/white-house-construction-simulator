# Bibliography and Image Sources

## Core historical sources

- White House Historical Association — history of the White House, construction, burning, restoration, and major renovations.
- National Park Service — White House and President's Park historical material.
- The White House official website — general institutional background.
- Wikimedia Commons — public-domain and freely licensed historical images.

## Hero image

- White House north and south sides, Wikimedia Commons.
- Used as a remote image in the first website version so the project can run without manually uploading image files.

## Notes for future work

Before adding each historical image permanently to the repository, verify:

1. License status.
2. Creator / photographer when known.
3. Year of creation.
4. Source URL.
5. Whether attribution is required.
